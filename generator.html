Email signature generator

Use this easy tool to create a professional, Johns Hopkins branded email signature.

 

Why use the generator?

  • Easy to use. We’ve already made the hard choices for you on what info is necessary in the signature and your login automatically adds directory information to get you started.
  • Responsive design. The signature’s clean design is responsive to mobile or desktop and displays consistently across the most commonly used email clients.
  • Professional and on brand. Email is the new stationery, so make sure you are representing yourself, your office, both internally and externally, professionally on brand.

Get Started

This tool is exclusively for Johns Hopkins University employees using an official Johns Hopkins (.edu) email account.

Verify and customize your information.

The generator automatically populates your directory information into each field. Just verify or update the information provided.

Click the generate button & enjoy.

Instantaneously, the generator will display your new signature. Simply click “Copy to clipboard,” paste into the signature display of your email client’s “signature” form, and save.

 

 

You’re all set! You can update your signature using this tool at any time as often as you need. Please note that any modifications to your signature once placed in Outlook will remove the formatting created in this special generator. Special signature badges and add-on images will be made available for special institution-wide campaigns meeting the specified requirements.

Pass it on. Share this tool with your fellow Johns Hopkins colleagues. Johns Hopkins Medicine employees also have access to an email signature creator.

Keep Your Signature on Brand

  • Email signatures should not promote or imply endorsement of a third party, or any unrelated externally-sponsored projects and gift-supported programs.
  • Do not include home phone numbers or personal e-mail addresses.
  • Do not link to outside third-party websites or logos.
  • Refrain from adding quotes, whimsical graphics, personal beliefs or statements.
  • No other visual marks, logos, badges or links should be added after theJH mark or outside of the Email Signature Generator.

How can I get an approved badge or image added to my signature?

The Johns Hopkins or divisional/school logo anchors the email signature and should not be altered or combined with any other mark. Any additional visual mark, badge, or add-ons must be approved by the Creative Strategy team in the Office of Communications and will be added directly to the email signature generator if it meets the following criteria:

  • Designed within appropriate scale, resolution, and required clear space for the JH logo.
  • Signifies an institution-wide effort with both internal and external audience appeal & immediate understanding/comprehension.
  • Must be aligned with and reflects the institutional values of Johns Hopkins University.
  • Must not be a commercial or for-profit promotion, or endorsement.
  • Meets Use of Name partnership, collaboration policy requirements.
  • Potentially an evergreen or long-term initiative (lasting at least a full academic term)
